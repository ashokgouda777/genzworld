Child Safety & Protection Standards

Effective Date: July 17, 2026

At GenZ World, protecting younger users and creating a secure, healthy environment is our absolute priority. This Child Safety document details the proactive measures, strict rules, and tools we employ to safeguard minors on our voice social network.

1. Age Restrictions & Gatekeeping

We enforce age-based access limits to separate adult interactions from general youth interactions:

  • Minimum Age Requirement (13+)

    You must be at least 13 years old to download and use the general features of GenZ World. The app is not designed or intended for anyone under 13.

  • Strict Mature Mode Lockout (18+)

    Mature modes—including "Flirt Mode"—are strictly limited to users who are 18 years of age or older. We utilize gatekeeping declarations and verify inputs to block minors from accessing mature matchmaking lobbies.

2. Zero Tolerance for Exploitation & Harassment

We maintain an absolute zero-tolerance policy towards any action that threatens the safety or well-being of children.

ALERT: Any form of child abuse, solicitation, grooming, exploitation, or sharing of sexually explicit content involving minors will result in immediate permanent device bans, server IP blocks, and direct reporting to law enforcement agencies (such as NCMEC).

To protect minors, we prohibit the following actions on our network:

  • Predatory Behavior & Grooming

    Any voice interactions or attempts to solicit personal identifiers, social media handles, or physical meetups with minors are strictly banned.

  • Abusive Voice Chat

    Bullying, hate speech, threats, harassment, or verbal abuse targeted at minor users is subject to permanent suspension.

3. Immediate Reporting & Moderation Tools

GenZ World equips users and community members with powerful, real-time safety controls:

  • Instant Blocking & Reporting

    During any live matchmaking call or inside any public audio room, users can press the reporting flag to immediately sever the call connection, block the peer, and submit a flag directly to moderators.

  • Rapid Moderation Actions

    Our backend automatically handles reports. Users who receive multiple rapid reports for abusive behavior or violation of community standards are permanently banned within minutes.

4. Safety Guidance for Parents & Guardians

Because GenZ World is designed around anonymous, real-time voice streaming (no records are stored on our servers to protect privacy), we recommend parents take the following actions:

  • Educate on Anonymity

    Teach your child never to share personal identifiers (like real names, phone numbers, home address, school name, or photos) on voice chats.

  • Device Level Controls

    We advise parents to utilize Google Play Parental Controls or iOS Screen Time restrictions to prevent children under 13 from downloading social and voice-matching communication apps.

  • Profile Reset/Deletion

    If you believe your child has set up an anonymous profile on the app, you can reset all app storage in the device's settings to wipe the anonymous user ID entirely, or contact us to delete their anonymous data state from our servers.

5. Regulatory Compliance & Contact

Our Child Safety guidelines are structured in compliance with the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)**, the **Information Technology (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, India**, and Google Play Store Developer Guidelines.

For any child safety concerns, parental deletion requests, or direct abuse reports, please contact our safety review board immediately at: safety@genzworld.app
